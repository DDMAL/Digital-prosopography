Abert, Anna Amalie, musicologist; b. Halle, Sept. 19, 1906. She studied at Berlin Univ. (Ph. D., 1934). In 1943 was appointed instructor at the Univ. of Kiel; 1950, prof, there; became assistant editor of the musical encyclopedia 'Die Musik in Geschichte und Gegenwart.' She et several collections of German choral music; published an important book on Monteverdi, Claudio Monteverdi und das musikalische Drama (1953).
