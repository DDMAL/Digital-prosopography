Agrell, Johan Joachim, Swedish composer; b. Loth, Feb. 1, 1701; d. Nuremberg, Jan. 19, 1765. He studied at Uppsala Univ.; later he was active in Kassel (1723in Nuremberg (from 1746). Among his published works are 5 concertos for cembalo and strings, 2 sonatas for violin and cembalo, 6 sonatas for cembalo solo, and pieces for cembalo in dance forms. His symphonies and cantatas are preserved in various European libraries (Stockholm, Uppsala, Brussels, Berlin, Konigsberg, Munich and Darmstadt).
