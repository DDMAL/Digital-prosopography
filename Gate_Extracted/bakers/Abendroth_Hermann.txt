Abendroth, Hermann, conductor and pedagogue; b. Frankfurt, Jan. 19, 1883; d. Jena, May 29, 1956. He studied in Munich (190011); Essen (1911also conducted at the Berlin State Opera (1923the Leipzig Cons, and conductor of the Gewandhaus Concerts. In 1947 he was appointed director of the Musikhochschule in Weimar; in 1949, music director of the Leipzig Radio.
