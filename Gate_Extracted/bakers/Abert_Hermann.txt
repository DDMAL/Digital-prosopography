Abert, Hermann, German music scholar; b. Stuttgart, March 25, 1871; d. there, Aug. 13, 1927. He studied with his father, Johann Joseph Abert; then at Tubingen Univ. {Dr. phil., 1897). He was Dozent of musical science at Halle Univ. (1902); prof, there (1909). In 1919 he was appointed prof, at Heidelberg Univ; 1920, at Leipzig Univ. (succeeding Hugo Riemann) ; 1923, at Berlin Univ.
