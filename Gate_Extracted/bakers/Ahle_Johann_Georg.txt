Ahle, Johann Georg, German organist and composer (son of Johann Rudolf Ahle) ; b. Muhlhausen, June, 1651 (baptized June 12); d. there, Dec. 1, 1706. He succeeded his father as organist in Muhlhausen, and was made poet laureate by Emperor Leopold I. Among Ahle's works published during his. lifetime are Musikalische FriihlingsSommer(1695of composition) ; Instrumentalische Friihlingsmusik (1676); Anmuthige zehn vierstimmige Violmany volumes of dances, sacred and secular songs.
