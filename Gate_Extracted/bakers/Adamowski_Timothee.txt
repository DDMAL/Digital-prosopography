Adamowski, Timothee, violinist; b. Warsaw, March 24, 1857; d. Boston, April 18, 1943. He studied in Warsaw and at the Paris Cons.; in 1879 gave concerts in the U.S. with Maurice Strakosch and Clara Louise Kellogg, and settled in Boston, where he taught at the New England Cons, (until 1933). In 1888 he organized the Adamowski String Quartet which gave about 30 concerts annually; he also conducted several summer seasons of popular concerts given by the Boston Symph. Orch.
