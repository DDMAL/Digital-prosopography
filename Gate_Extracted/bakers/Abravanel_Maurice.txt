Abravanel, Maurice, conductor; b. Saloniki (Greece), Jan. 6, 1903. He studied at Lausanne Univ., and later in Berlin. Leaving Germany in 1933 he conducted ballet in Paris and London; toured Australia with the British National Opera Co. (193435). Conducted at Metropolitan Opera (1936(1940of the Utah State Symph. Orch. at Salt Lake City.
