Aerts (ahrts), Egide, Belgian flutist; b. Boom, near Antwerp, March 1, 1822; d. Brussels, June 9, 1853. A precocious musician, he studied as a child at the Brussels Cons.; gave a concert in Paris at the age of 15. In 1847 he was appointed teacher of flute at the Brussels Cons. He wrote numerous works for flute, most of which remain in manuscript.
