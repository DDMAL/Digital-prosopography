Aguilar (ahham, English pianist and composer of Spanish descent; b. London, Aug. 23, 1824; d. there Feb. 18, 1904. He wrote a collection of canons and fugues as preparatory exercises for the playing of Bach; also composed 3 symphonies, 2 overtures, much chamber music and the operas, Wave King (1855) and The Bridal Wreath (1863).
