Adorno, Theodor (real name Wiesengrund), music theorist; b. Frankfurt, Sept. 11, 1903. He studied with Sekles in Frankfurt and Alban Berg in Vienna. Was music critic in Frankfurt; then instructor at the Univ. there. He emigrated to the U.S. in 1934; was connected with radio research at Princeton (1938ifornia. In 1950 he returned to Frankfurt and resumed his professorship there. Adorno published Philosophie der neuen Musik (Tubingen, 1949) and numerous articles on music in relation to society (radio, jazz, etc. ) ; in his early writings he used the name Wiesengrund
