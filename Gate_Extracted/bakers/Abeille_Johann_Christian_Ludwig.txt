Abeille, Johann Christian Ludwig, German organist and composer; b. Bayreuth, Feb. 20, 1761; d. Stuttgart, March 2, 1838. He was educated in Stuttgart, and was leader of the private orchestra of the Duke of Wiirttemberg ; in 1802 became court music director, retiring in 1832. He published several albums of songs which found their way into vocal anthologies and wrote 2 light operas Amor und Psyche (1801) and Peter und Annchen (1809); also composed concerted music for small groups, and harpsichord pieces.
