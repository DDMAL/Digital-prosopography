Abbado, Marcello, pianist and composer; b. Milan, Oct. 7, 1926. He studied at the Cons, in Milan with Gavazzeni (piano) and Ghedini (composition), graduating in 1947. In 1951 he was appointed instructor at the Cons, of Venice. He has written a cantata Ciapo (1945); Lento e Rondo for violin and piano (1949) and piano pieces.
